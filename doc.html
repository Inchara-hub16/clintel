Dr.Vanitha N

Specialty: Cardiologist

Hospital: Baptist Hospital,Banglore

Education: MBBS,DNB -Gen. Medicine,DNB- Cardiology

Experience of 16 Years

In a video message from an interventional cardiologist Vanitha N at Bangalore Baptist Hospital, the importance of managing hypertension is emphasized on the eve of World Hypertension Day. The cardiologist highlights the growing incidence of hypertension in India and globally, associating it with severe complications such as heart attacks, heart failure, strokes, and kidney issues. The physician stresses the necessity of regular check-ups and medication management to effectively control hypertension. Alongside medical intervention, lifestyle modifications are crucial, including regular exercise, reduced salt intake, avoidance of junk foods, and managing other health conditions like diabetes and high cholesterol. Special attention is directed toward the rising hypertension rates among women, particularly during pregnancy, underlining the need for vigilance and control to ensure the safety of both mother and baby. The video concludes with reminders to reduce stress through physical activity and meditation to help manage hypertension effectively.
